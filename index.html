Our Donate Plan

All the funds collected for tackling the flood situation will be channelled towards the recovery
of flood victims. With your support and dedication of our 50000 pool of volunteers
across the country, we will drive to respond to the urge to save lives.

Food for everyone

Due to the flooding, the people of rural areas can't eat food properly. So it will be one of the main to goal to reach quality food to the people who are suffering. We will also provie pure water to each families.

Shelter for a family

Through our donation money we will create temporary shelters. It is very important to give shelter to those families who has lost their houses due to flooding. Also after the flooding declines we will help them to restore their house.

Medicine for a family

Providing medicine to the people who have suffered from this flood is one of our main goal. During this disaster people suffer from various disease, specially the children and older people suffers the most.
